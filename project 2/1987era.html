The Lakers vs Celtics: The Greatest Sports Rivalry Ever

By: Bridget Reyes

The 1984-1887 era of basketball
The 1984-87 Finals era. This 3 year era brought the best basketball ever. With the most dominate teams competing one another is unforgettable. Both teams had the best players ranging from Larry Bird, Magic Johnson, Kareem-Abdul-Jabbar, and more. In the 1987 season of basketball the Lakers were able to surpass the Celtics and dodged Game 7. Gane 7 is known to be sudden death where you go big or you go home, literally. Since the Lakers where able to dodge Game 7 they still knew that no matter if Game 7 were to happen they still would play their best basketball in order to win a title ring.
In Game 6 of the 1987 series between the Lakers and the Celtics, the Lakers were able to beat the Celtics (106-93).
