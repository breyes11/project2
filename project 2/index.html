The Lakers vs Celtics: The Greatest Sports Rivalry Ever

By: Bridget Reyes

A Brief Description
Throughout the entire history of sports there is always a rivalry between two teams. No matter how it all started they will always have a history that no one can ever get passed by. One of those greatest rivalries started in basketball, The Lakers vs The Celtics. This rivalry has shaped basketball itself and no matter if your a fan or not you will always side with one. This rivalry has divided families, relationships, or any possible friendships.
